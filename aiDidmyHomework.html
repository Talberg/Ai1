Children have more information at their fingertips than scholars had 30 years ago. We live in the Age of Information, where any fact that you can possibly want to know is just a search away. I feel that there are conversations happening in the classrooms about not always going to have Ai to help you. This is something that falls in the same category of "You will not always have a calculator in your pocket".

Is it ok to have Ai help with your homework?

Yes, Ai is a tool, you should be able to use Ai to help with school work because you will most likely use some from of Ai in any profession that you choose. But you have to check the work of the Ai and make sure the info that it gives you is correct and not just confident. This is similar to a parent helping a child in my opinion.

Ai must be used as tool to help with homework or else we are not learning. If we use Ai as an alternative to critical thinking this could cause a brain drain or even a mass misinformation being the base of a generation's reality.

It is still the parents job to regulate the use of the internet and technology by their child. It is hard to keep up with the new technology but to keep your children safe you need to at least understand the basics of Ai
