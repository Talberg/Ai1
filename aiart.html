Can Ai Create Art?

In my opinion Ai does create art and things created with Ai are art. Art is subjective. Just because the paint bush was a prompt does not mean the art is invalid. Art evolves over time like anything, this is just a new era of art. People said that digital art was not art for a while because it was not created by the artist but created by the computer. Fully Generated Art

With fully generated content there is a lot more debate. In order for these Ai to create images from words they have to be trained and in the training lies the problem. Should the artists that created the data for the training be compensated? Or is this just like any other human that can learn how to paint by watching Bob Ross? In my opinion the use of Ai to generate images is a tool that will help many people break into fields that they would have never been able to. But my main issue is that the data sets are still a black box. We do not know how many artists work that do not want to be included are in the training of these models.

This is the prompt "Arch in the desert next to a lake" put in to the Starryai Generator. In the Terms of Service they say that all my creations are mine and I own them. "All generated media is allowed for commercial and non-commercial usage..."

If you are going to use Ai generated content then you should read the Terms of Service of any tool you use. This could come to bite you if you don't own your creations.
