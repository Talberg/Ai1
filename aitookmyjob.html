One of the things that will arise from this Ai Revolution is the amount of jobs that it could cause to disappear over night. This page talks about the issues that we will face in the very near future dealing with Ai putting thousands of people out of work. There is a section that talks about jobs that are currently being lost do to Ai. I also propose my opinions on the best steps that we can take to prevent disaster

Who will lose their jobs?

I see that a lot of publications are saying that coders will be displaces but I disagree, coders will just use the ai as a tool in their workflow. The real issues tha I forsee are more in the creative areas. Why would we pay an artist to design a new logo when ai can make us the most optimized image? Advertizing will become nothing but ai serving up personalized ads. Lawyers will see a hit to all fields like risk management and TOS.

If we just let large companies use ai to replace large amounts of the workforce with ai then we will need solutions to tax and redistribute the profits with some system of universal basic income. If we do not take care of the issue then the rich will just get richer and the poor will starve.
